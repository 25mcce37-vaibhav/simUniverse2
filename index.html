ABOUT SIMU-VERSE

SimuVerse is an interactive physics simulation platform designed to help students visualize and understand complex concepts like Wave Optics, Projectile Motion, and Electrostatics.

Featuring NOVA, our built-in AI Physics Tutor, users can learn through guided lessons, interact with real-time simulations, and ask questions using text or voice!
